Jie Ren

Associate Professor

School of Artificial Intelligence and Computer Science, Shaanxi Normal University (陕西师范大学)

National-Local Joint Engineering Research Center for New Network Intelligent Information Services, Northwest University (新型网络智能信息服务国家地方联合工程研究中心, 西北大学)

I work on compiler and systems research for efficient, reliable, and practical computing. My recent work focuses on compiler and program optimisation, binary analysis and decompilation, efficient large language model inference and training, and mobile or edge AI systems. I am particularly interested in building working systems that combine program analysis, machine learning, and systems techniques to improve performance in real deployment settings.

Research interests

  • Compiler and program optimisation: LLVM-based optimisation, auto-tuning, cross-platform performance portability, and AI-assisted compilers.
  • Binary analysis and decompilation: lifting optimised binaries to compiler IR, program understanding, and LLM-assisted reverse engineering.
  • Efficient AI systems: low-latency LLM inference, lightweight training, and edge or mobile deployment.
  • Mobile and edge computing: performance, energy efficiency, and system support for intelligent applications.

Students and opportunities

I am looking for motivated students who enjoy programming, systems research, and building real prototypes. If you are interested in compilers, systems, mobile or edge AI, or LLM systems, feel free to email me your CV and transcript.
